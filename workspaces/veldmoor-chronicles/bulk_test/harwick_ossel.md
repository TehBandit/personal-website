HARWICK OSSEL — character notes

Harwick Ossel is a Senior Surveyor of the Admiralty and currently serves as the deputy head of the Cartographic Division. He is fifty-three, heavyset, with a deliberate manner that reads as gravitas to those who admire him and as obstruction to those who have worked against him. Ossel came up through the navigational corps the conventional way — thirty years of service, careful alliances, and a talent for being indispensable to whoever was making decisions at any given time. His skill is organizational, knowing where things are filed, who controls access to what, and which requests can be delayed indefinitely without formal objection. A complaint filed against him by Rella Corse alleged that he had been supplying altered survey data to a private concern, but the investigation found no corroborating evidence. Ossel lives in the upper district of Tiveck in rented accommodation considerably nicer than a deputy surveyor's salary would normally support. He is a member of two Admiralty social committees and is regarded as a capable administrator. There is a standing rumor — unverified — that he has a private arrangement with someone in the Deepwater Guild, which would constitute a significant breach of the Compact of Survey Rights.
