SHŌYŌ HINATA — character notes

Shōyō Hinata is a passionate and energetic volleyball player who serves as the main protagonist of the Haikyū!! series. Initially a first-year student at Karasuno High, he plays as a middle blocker for the Boys' Volleyball Team. Despite his short stature, Hinata is known for his incredible jumping ability and tenacity on the court. Inspired by the 'Small Giant,' a former ace of Karasuno, Hinata is determined to become a formidable player despite his height. Over the series, he evolves from a nervous and inexperienced player into a key member of his team, known for his quick attacks and ability to motivate those around him. His journey takes him from Karasuno High to playing for the MSBY Black Jackals in the V-League and eventually joining the Japan Men's National Volleyball Team during the 2021 Olympics. As of 2022, Hinata is a member of Asas São Paulo in the Brazil Super League.
