FEN CALDRA — character notes

Background: Fen is in his mid-40s. Born in Tiveck, grew up on the docks, has never lived more than two miles from the waterfront. Spent his twenties working legitimate cargo vessels. Spent his thirties expanding his understanding of what "cargo" can mean. He now operates a small private transport service — two vessels, a rotating crew of four he trusts, and a reputation in certain circles for reliability and discretion. He is not violent. He is careful, which is a more sustainable quality.

Current operations: He runs cargo through the Salt Warren, a network of tidal caves and excavated chambers beneath the eastern cliff face of Tiveck. The Warren has been used for gray-market storage for generations — Fen didn't build it, he just maintains the right relationships to use it. He stores things there for people who don't want certain items in official warehouses. He asks what things are only when he needs to know for handling purposes.

The Sunken Ledger: Fen knows what it is. He found it six years ago in a sealed crate he was clearing from a Warren chamber the previous operator had abandoned. He read enough to understand its value — and its danger. He has not sold it, shown it to anyone, or destroyed it. It is still in the Warren, in a location only he knows. He has thought about what to do with it for six years and has not yet decided.

Relationship with Orrus Vane: Complex. Fen has leverage over Orrus in the form of the Warden's Seal — a forged customs document that Fen commissioned and then used to implicate Orrus in a cargo irregularity years ago. Orrus knows Fen has it. This is not a friendship. It is a functional arrangement that has kept both of them out of serious trouble for a long time. Fen would prefer not to use it. He keeps it because having it is useful.

Relationship with Maren Ashveil: He finds her interesting, which is unusual. Most of his clients are easy to read. Maren is harder. He's transported her twice without issue and done two small errands for her. He has not told her about the Ledger. He is fairly confident she is looking for it. He has not decided what to do about this.

Personality: Unhurried. Speaks in short sentences and waits to see how people respond to silence before continuing. Generous with small things — he'll buy a round, help carry something, remember a name. Careful with large ones. Doesn't make promises he hasn't already planned to keep.

Physical details: Stocky, weathered, gray coming into dark hair. Missing the top joint of the index finger on his right hand — lost it to a winch, years ago. Usually has a cup of something warm nearby that he forgets to drink.
